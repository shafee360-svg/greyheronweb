Our Mission

The Heart of the Stories


Maldivian folklore offers a rich window into the culture, language, beliefs, and everyday life of the Maldivian people. Passed down orally for generations, these stories reflect the unique worldview of a fragile island nation.

Our collection features 14 titles retold with great respect for their originality. We believe in the value of human creativity — no AI tools are used in the creation of our artwork. Each illustration is crafted with care and cultural sensitivity.
