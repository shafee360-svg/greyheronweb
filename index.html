Our Mission

The Heart of the Stories

Maldivian folklore offers a rare window into the history, culture, and early way of life of the Maldivian people. Passed down orally for generations, these stories reflect the unique worldview of a fragile island nation.

Our collection features 14 titles retold with great respect for their originality. No AI was used in the writing or the artwork; the whole process is intentionally AI-less. Each illustration is crafted with care and cultural sensitivity.
